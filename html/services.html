Additional Services

Delivery

Deliverying wooden furniture without scratching or damaging it can be tricky.

We offer delivery services that include packaging and delivery to the doors or even inside the building. This way, you don't have to worry about lifting heavy stuff or going through a narrow entrance with a big piece of furniture.

Installation

All of our orders are personalized and made following directions from our customers; therefore, some of the items we make might not be as easy to install as to put Lego pieces together.

Per your request, we can send our professionals to install any item at any time, either right after the delivery, or later, if you are not ready yet to have the item installed upon delivery.

Restoration

If you have a piece that has been in your family for generations or you are just so attached to some old and worn out piece that you just can't throw away, our restorative services are perfect for you.

We can refresh the paint or cover and fix broken parts or scratches.
