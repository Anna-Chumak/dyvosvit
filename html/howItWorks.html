Our Process

Creating a unique piece takes time and patience. And here you will find out more about the stages this process includes.

1. You contact us

2. You explain what you want

3. We take all the measurements

4. We design a piece

5. Choose the material and color

6. We start working on your piece

7. We cut out the wood

8. Glue parts together

9. We sand it down

10. We paint

11. We polish

12. We deliver and install
